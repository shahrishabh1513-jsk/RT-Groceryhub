Our Story

RT GroceryHub was founded with a simple mission: to make fresh, quality groceries accessible to every Indian household at affordable prices. What started as a small local delivery service has now grown into a trusted online grocery platform serving thousands of happy customers.

We believe that fresh food should be just a click away. That's why we've built a robust supply chain that ensures farm-fresh produce reaches your doorstep within hours of harvest.
