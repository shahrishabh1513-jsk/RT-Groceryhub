Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers! Discover quick solutions for a seamless shopping experience.

1. How do I place an order online?

Placing an order is simple! Browse our catalog, add items to cart, and proceed to checkout. We offer multiple payment options including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and secure online banking.

2. What delivery options are available?

We offer Same-Day Delivery (order before cutoff), Scheduled Delivery (choose a convenient time slot), and Express Delivery for urgent orders. Delivery times vary by location.

3. How do I track my order?

After order confirmation, you'll receive an email/SMS with a tracking link. You can also log into your account and view "My Orders" section for real-time updates.

4. What is your return and refund policy?

If you receive damaged or incorrect items, contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We'll arrange for replacement, refund, or credit as per your preference.
