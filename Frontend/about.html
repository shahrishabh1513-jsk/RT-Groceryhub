About RT Groceryhub

India's fastest growing online grocery store, delivering fresh products to your doorstep

Our Story

Founded in 2024, RT Groceryhub started with a simple mission - to make fresh, quality groceries accessible to every Indian household at affordable prices. What began as a small delivery service in one neighborhood has now grown into one of India's most trusted online grocery platforms.

We partner directly with farmers, local producers, and trusted brands to ensure that every product on our platform meets our high standards of quality and freshness. Our state-of-the-art supply chain ensures that your groceries reach you within 30 minutes of ordering.
