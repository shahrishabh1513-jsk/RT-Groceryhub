FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

🛒 How do I place an order online?

Placing an order is simple! Browse our catalog by category or use the search bar to find specific items. Add your chosen products to the cart, review your order, and proceed to checkout. We offer multiple payment options including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and Cash on Delivery.

🚚 What delivery options are available?

We offer Same-Day Delivery (order before 2 PM), Scheduled Delivery (choose a convenient time slot), and Express 30-Minute Delivery for urgent orders. Delivery charges may vary based on your location and order value. Orders above ₹499 get free delivery!

📍 Do you deliver to my area?

We currently deliver across Vadodara city including Subhanpura, Alkapuri, Fatehgunj, Karelibaug, and surrounding areas. Enter your PIN code on the website or app to check if we deliver to your location. We're constantly expanding our delivery network!

📦 How do I track my order?

After your order is confirmed, you'll receive an email and/or SMS with a tracking link. Simply click the link to view real-time updates on your delivery status. You can also log into your account and access the "My Orders" section for tracking details.

🔄 What is your return and refund policy?

If you receive damaged, expired, or incorrect items, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement, refund, or credit according to your preference. We aim to resolve all issues within 24 hours.
